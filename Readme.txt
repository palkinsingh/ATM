1.Open ATM folder in VSC and in terminal Run nodemon index or node index.js
2.Go to your Webbrowser and enter http://localhost:8000
3.You can check your inital balance to start with and come back to home
4.click on cash withdrawal and enter the amount you want to withdraw.
5.Keep in mind amound should not be'0' nor greater than balance and only in multiples.
6. let's say you entered 9700.
7.A alert box will appear to confirm your payment press 'ok'
8.Amount you entered will be deducted from your balance.
9. A new alert box will apper showing your new balance.
10.if 9700 is the amount you will get 4:2000notes,3:500notes and 1:200 note.
End of flow.
